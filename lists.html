c. Description List: Facts about Nepal

Capital
Kathmandu, the capital of Nepal, is a vibrant city nestled in the Kathmandu Valley. Known for its rich cultural heritage, the city boasts ancient temples, bustling markets, and a harmonious blend of tradition and modernity, making it a captivating destination for visitors.
Official Language
Nepali, also known as Gorkhali or Nepalese, is the official language of Nepal. It belongs to the Indo-Aryan language family and is spoken by the majority of the population. The script used is Devanagari. Nepali plays a crucial role in Nepal's cultural and national identity.
Highest Mountain
Mount Everest, the world's highest peak, stands at an elevation of 29,032 feet (8,848 meters) in the Himalayas, on the border between Nepal and China (Tibet). A coveted destination for mountaineers, Everest's majestic summit attracts adventurers from around the globe, embodying the spirit of human perseverance and exploration.
National Flower
Rhododendron is a genus of flowering plants, including both evergreen and deciduous shrubs and trees. Known for their vibrant and showy flowers, they are found in various climates worldwide. In Nepal, the rhododendron is the national flower, symbolizing the country's natural beauty. These flowers come in a range of colors and are often seen in the Himalayan region, adding to the scenic landscapes.
